    Structure and History

    The Department of Science and Innovation provides the majority of funding for the Centre for Artificial Intelligence Research (CAIR), a dispersed South African research network with nine established and two emergent research groups spread across eight institutions (DSI). Virtually, the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research serves as its host and coordinator (CSIR).
